Return Policy

At Fruitables, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a 30-Day Money Back Guarantee on eligible products.

1. 30-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can request a return or refund within 30 days from the date of delivery.

2. Eligible for Return

Returns are accepted only if:

  • You received damaged or spoiled fruits or vegetables
  • The product delivered was incorrect
  • The product quality does not meet expectations

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned due to hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Cut fruits and vegetables
  • Highly perishable items after being opened
  • Products stored incorrectly after delivery

4. How to Request a Return

To request a return, please contact us with your order number and product photos within 30 days.
